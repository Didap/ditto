# Pricing — Ditto

## Free
- Price: $0/month
- Limits: 3 design extractions/month, 1 active project
- Features: Design extraction, DESIGN.md export, CSS variables export, 6 live preview templates
- Sign up: https://ditto.design/register

## Pro
- Price: $19/month (billed annually) | $24/month (billed monthly)
- Limits: Unlimited extractions, unlimited projects
- Features: Everything in Free, React component export, Push to Figma, priority extraction queue, mood blending with fine-tuning
- Sign up: https://ditto.design/register?plan=pro

## Team
- Price: $49/month (billed annually) | $59/month (billed monthly)
- Limits: Unlimited extractions, unlimited projects, 5 team members
- Features: Everything in Pro, shared design library, team design history, priority support
- Sign up: https://ditto.design/register?plan=team
